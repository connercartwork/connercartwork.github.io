The Dunes of Tunes

The Dunes of Tunes is a side-scrolling platformer with a rhythm game element during combat. It was made using Visual Studio and C#, and was developed by a development team of four people. I was primarily in charge of the combat system, and served as a designer, producer, and programmer. You play as a successful jazz musician, Jaz Zee, on his quest for some ancient sheet music rumored to be hidden in one of the runes of the Muze Desert. Unfortunately for him, the desert he finds himself in is incredibly dangerous, and it might prove more of a challenge to get this treasure than he thought.
